Anomaly Detection Summary

During log analysis, unusual authentication behavior was identified. Multiple failed login attempts (Event ID 4625) were observed from the same source IP address within a short time period.

Such patterns may indicate a brute-force attack or unauthorized login attempt. Repeated authentication failures can signal malicious activity, especially when followed by outbound network communication.

Additionally, outbound DNS traffic to external IP addresses was observed shortly after the authentication attempts. This could indicate potential command-and-control communication or reconnaissance activity.

Monitoring anomalies such as unusual login times, repeated authentication failures, and abnormal network connections helps SOC analysts detect potential security incidents early.

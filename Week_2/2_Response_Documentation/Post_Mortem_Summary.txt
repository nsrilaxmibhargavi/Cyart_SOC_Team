Post-Mortem Summary

A brute-force SSH attack was detected by the monitoring system. The alert was triggered after multiple failed login attempts from a suspicious IP address. The SOC analyst investigated authentication logs and confirmed repeated login failures.

No successful login attempts were identified and no system compromise occurred.

To reduce the risk of similar attacks in the future, the following improvements are recommended:
- Implement SSH rate limiting
- Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Use SSH key-based authentication instead of passwords
